`cPhyProb` Frequently asked questions
=====================================

1. What is cPhyProb? 
    cPhyProb is a package implementing some probability calculations used in
    calculating likelihoods on phylogenies. Computationally intensive steps
    are programmed as C-extensions to python.

2. Where is the homepage for this package?
    http://people.ku.edu/~mtholder/cPhyProb/
